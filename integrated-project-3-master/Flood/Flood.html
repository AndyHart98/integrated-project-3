Flood Data is displayed in 5 different categories,
County - Showing the Area of the flood in England.
Message - A Small Description of the flood, If undefined is shown then there is no description of the flood at current time.
Severity Level - Saying how serious the flood.
Is Tidal? - True stating that the water is still rising and False stating that it is not.
Severity level number - A number range of 1 to 4 stating how bad the flood is 4 being very serious and 1 being the least serious.
