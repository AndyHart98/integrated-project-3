The information displayed about the tornado is taken from the API and displayed onto the wepage, while the latitude and longtitude coordindates taken from the api are used to place a marker on a Google Maps map. Additionally the coordinates are then displayed in the top left hand corner of the map.
The coordinates are then used to plot the point of origin on the map, however they are taken in from the API in reverse order, longitude first.
